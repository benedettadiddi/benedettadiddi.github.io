Cashlab
Brand Identity

CashLab is a start-up developed in Tuscany, thanks to the initiative of experts in the textile industry who wanted to launch an innovative, eco-friendly product.

Innovation, sustainability and tradition are the core values of the brand and the aim was to enhance them by building up a solid identity starting from finding a suitable name for the brand, to the development of a website.
